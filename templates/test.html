ICARB-2005

Coconut Shell Granular Activated Carbon

ICARB-2005 is available in different grades.

ICarb 2005 Granular Activated Carbon is ideal for most Air purification purposes. Made from selected Grades of coconut shell, its superior level of hardness and unique micro pores, offers excellent adsorption of toxic and micro particulates in air.
